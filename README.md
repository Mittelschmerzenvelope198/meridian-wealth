## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What if the application does not load?
Ensure your internet connection is active. Check that your Windows firewall allows the application to communicate with your internal databases.

Can I export the data?
Yes, use the Export button located in the results pane. You can save your documents as PDF or Excel files.

How does the agent identify data?
The software uses a backend engine called LangChain. This engine connects to your official financial data feeds and ensures the information remains accurate to your company policies.

Is my data kept on the internet?
The core agent logic runs locally on your workstation. It only retrieves specific data points from your authorized servers when you initiate a search.
